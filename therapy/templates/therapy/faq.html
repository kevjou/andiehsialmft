What state do you practice in?

I am currently licensed to practice in California and only accept patients living in California.

Do you take insurance?

No, I only accept cash payment.

Do you provide in-person and virtual sessions?

I find the flexibility of virtual sessions allows more freedom for me to meet my clients where they are. At the moment, I do not offer in-person sessions.

What is your current rate?

$200 per clinical hour. However, I always discuss with potential clients in the case of special exceptions.

Is it more expensive uninsured?

As I adhere to the federal regulation 45 CFR 149.610 Requirements for provision of good faith estimates of expected charges for uninsured (or self-pay) individuals, you can rest assured that my rates are set in a way that is reasonable and fair for all parties involved.

How long does therapy take?

Therapy is an individual, growth-oriented journey which focuses on achieving developmental checkpoints, which we discuss and work towards in our sessions. Whether you feel ready to move on, or if you want to make therapy a regular part of your life, I want to work with you towards reaching those goals.
